UNIVERSITY OF GUADALAJARA ALUMNI

We love schools because we are always learning!

Working with schools has always been interesting because we can contribute more to the community we live in, plus we love all process that involves the creation of human knowledge. The Universidad de Guadalajara approached to us with the idea of making an app that would allow alumni to respond surveys; while working with them, we got to the conclusion that the solution was to create an app with contextual content according to user's interests so they would keep the app in their mobiles. Humaind collaborated with the design of the app and the software development was made by other agency.

UX

We concentrated in a fast experience, that at the same time it would meet the university’s targets and cover its needs. That was the main challenge with this project. With the positive reinforcements we accomplished with the interaction within the app, we kept the users active for a longer period of time. The project was presented with Android and iOS versions, as well as an administration system which facilitated the use for the UdeG team.
