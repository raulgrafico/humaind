BIGAPPETIT

Are you at home or the office with no choices left to eat?

With Bigappetit you can order food from any location. It was a big challenge, the different scenarios and configuration of each order made us work with a wide range of options for the App users. At the same time, the design for iOS and Android versions made us look for a solution that would adapt to both platforms to offer the same experience to all users.

UX

iOS and Android, designing a project for both platforms implies a new way of thinking that gives both users the same experience; we worked hard to achieve that effect, always paying special attention in the details, so the project would have the best user experience and at the same time to be attractive for the participating restaurants.

UI
