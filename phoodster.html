UX/UI

PHOODSTER

Organising food recipes based on your favourite supermarket offers.

They approached us from Sweden with the need to solve this project. There is no doubt we all love to eat delicious food and better if we can do it getting special offers from our favourite super market. Phoodster combines both elements in one App and it’s about to be a huge success in Europe!

UX

We gave the app’s interface a look inspired in the project’s branding, a very elegant and sober aspect but at the same time gave the correct readability and navigability of the contents, which makes Phoodster a beautiful and enjoyable app to interact with.
